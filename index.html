Our Tiny Love Story: "We Were Miles Apart But Not Our Hearts"

Modern Love in miniature, featuring auggie-submitted stories of no more than 100 or maybe sometimes more words.




By Augustine Ng

Feb 10, 2022


SIGN UP FOR AUGGIE TIMES: Get stories that capture the wonders of our love, how to teleport and the secret of staying so cute.


Disco Ball-ing My Eyes Out

It was an exhausting day and I was feeling lonely and down. Matthew told me to put on my favourite record and lie down in the darkness soaking in the music with all my senses. At one point I switched on a light to reflect off the disco ball I had hanging in my room. Being the attentionally challenged zoomer I am, I filmed a short clip of the ball spinning to post on instagram. The next morning at work, a new face appeared in my messages. "Omg I MISS having a disco ball at home. Such a mood booster" And just like that, my mood got a little boost as well.

So Do You Like Me? Or Do You Like Like Me?

March 20. Well I guess if we're being historically accurate, it was Friday March 19 night for me, and Saturday March 20 morning for you. I had gone out earlier to pick up some goodies for our scheduled ~*video call~* that night. I still remember going to pick up "The White Book" from the North York Centre library and realizing how much of an influence you were having on my life already. I really wanted those muscat grapes because they made me think of Korea (/you) and I thought they'd be appropriate call-food. I ended up settling for green grapes instead

After dinner, I scurried off to my room and shut the door behind me. I must admit, I was a little nervous. Phone calls in general make me anxious, so a video call, especially one with a girl I have kind of a massive crush on, was peaking my anxiety. But it was a good anxious. Anyways, we call and it's chill and cute and fun, as always. We cover topics from here to there everything under the sun. We giggle and smile and interrupt each other awkwardly, then wait for the other to start talking first because we're just so polite.

Hours go by and we don't even notice. Eventually it's getting a little too late, or early, for me and I need to go to sleep. You want to bring something up but I must keep changing the subject and hey, maybe you were nervous too. Eventually you hit me with something along the lines of "one more thing..." And the rest is history~ We've come back to this story time and time again, I'm sure you know it by heart now. The start of something so new, so tender, precious, deeply meaningful and rewarding had just begun...



i love you.

April 24. Night time. My whole family was over for dinner. "The child" and I want to say Greg and Erin as well? My memory is not too great. We had pizza and me and you made jokes about Little Italy via msgs. I remember (with the help of screenshots) that the discord wanted to watch Greener Grass that night. Since I was tied up with *socializing* you popped in to start watching first. You sent me a message asking if I was joining or not. I teased you and told you I was too tired, but I soon joined.

So we watch the movie together with the discord gang (gang!) and the whole time we're texting each other about how spooky it is and how we wish we were next to each other. It ends and I make up something to leave first, you're still trapped watching the analysis with them. You wait awhile and then it's US time again. I believe we watch the movie beginning again just to catch what we missed. At this point I'm pretty lethargic but I just can't ever get enough of time with you (yes, still!) I fall asleep a couple of times and then end up in bed with all the lights off.

So it's early Sunday morning April 25 for me by now. I'm wiggling around in bed, talking to you analog mode. I'm struggling to stay awake but our conversation keeps getting cuter and cuter (I'm assuming.) We're really getting into love-y dove-y territory and something stirs in my heart. Like a baby bird having to take its first little leap to fly, I tell you I love you. You're happy I went first, our love admittance feud finally ended, you tell me you love me too. My heart is beating so fast, again something so new feels so right.
